AI Foundation Course — Day 1

1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, decision-making and problem solving.

Hindi: AI कंप्यूटर विज्ञान की वह शाखा है जो मशीनों को मानव बुद्धि जैसे कार्य करने में सक्षम बनाती है।

2. Basic Idea Behind AI

AI simulates human thinking using algorithms and data. It learns from data and improves over time.

3. Historical Roots of AI

The roots of AI trace back to ancient philosophical ideas. Modern AI began in the 20th century with developments in mathematics and computing.

4. Alan Turing & Foundation of AI

Alan Turing proposed the concept of machine intelligence in 1950 and introduced the famous Turing Test.

5. Dartmouth Conference (1956)

The term "Artificial Intelligence" was officially coined by John McCarthy in 1956.

6. Early AI Scientists

Alan Turing, John McCarthy, Marvin Minsky, Herbert Simon and Allen Newell.

7. Neural Networks

Neural networks are computing systems inspired by the human brain that process data using interconnected nodes.

8. Important Milestones

  • 1997 — IBM Deep Blue defeated Garry Kasparov
  • 2011 — IBM Watson won Jeopardy
  • 2016 — AlphaGo defeated world champion
  • 2020s — Rise of Generative AI like ChatGPT

9. AI as Fifth Industrial Revolution

AI integrates intelligence into machines and transforms industries like healthcare, education, finance and defense.

10. AI & Job Market

AI may replace some jobs but will also create many new opportunities in the future.

11. Fields Where AI is Used

  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Finance
  • Agriculture
  • Defense
  • Content Creation

12. Ethical AI

AI must be used responsibly to avoid bias, misinformation and misuse.
