DAY-2

Using AI and Large Language Models in Academics
  • How AI can support your studies?
    AI tools like ChatGPT can help you understand complex topics, summarize texts, and explain concepts in simple terms.
  • Example: Summarizing class notes
    Prompt: "Summarize these notes on [topic] in simple terms."
  • Uploading your syllabus for AI assistance
    You can upload a syllabus PDF and ask AI to remember key details.
  • Retrieving syllabus info quickly
    Prompt: "From my syllabus, what is the exam date for [subject]?"
  • Creating a study plan with AI
    Prompt: "Create a 2-week study plan to cover my syllabus."
  • Using AI for exam preparation
    Prompt: "Create practice questions based on my syllabus."
  • Browsing government exam syllabus
    Prompt: "Find the syllabus for [exam] and help plan strategy."
  • Using AI to draft assignments
    Prompt: "Help me draft an essay outline on [topic]."
Using Deep Research Feature in ChatGPT
  • Define your research topic clearly.
  • Use deep research mode for academic responses.
  • Ask for literature review.
  • Identify research gaps.
  • Generate research objectives & hypothesis.
  • Ask for methodology design.
  • Request citation format APA/MLA.
  • Draft chapter-wise thesis structure.
  • Ask for data interpretation help.
  • Always cross-verify sources.
Important Academic Note

AI assists research but does not replace critical thinking. Always verify information from trusted academic sources.
